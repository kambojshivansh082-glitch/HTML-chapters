zomato is a restaurant search and discovery service founded in 2008 by Deepinder Goyal and Pankaj Chaddah. It provides information, menus and user-reviews of restaurants, and also has food delivery options from partner restaurants in select cities. The company operates in 24 countries across the world, including the United States, India, the United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, and several countries in the Middle East and Southeast Asia.
